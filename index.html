Zelda NES Recreated with Custom Level

Play Now

An authentic recreation of the original 1986 NES Legend of Zelda's first dungeon + an original custom level where you can capture the Keese enemy for a series of puzzle platforming challenges.

The game was developed using Unity and C#. For the recreation of the original game I worked on implementing Link's movement, the health and damage systems, and the Stalfos, Keese, Goriya, and Bladetrap enemies, amongst other tasks. For the custom level I focused on building out tools like the pressure plates and Keese spawner, and collaborated on the level design and progression

Controls: Movement - WASD/Arrow keys, Primary Attack - X, Secondary Attack - Z, Swap Weapons - Space, Start Custom Level - 4
